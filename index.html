SOME OF THE MOST ICONIC GUITARS IN HISTORY

On this page I'll be taking you through some of the most iconic guitars in history :)

GIBSON LES PAUL

The Gibson Les Paul is a heavy "thick" sounding guitar created by Les Paul. It is arguably the most iconic looking guitar in history.
It is used by players that wish to have a bite to their sound, and was used by many of the original hard rock guitarists such as Jimmy Page and Slash.



FENDER STRATOCASTER

The Fender Stratocaster is a lighter "thin" sounding guitar created by Leo Fender.
It is used by players that wish to have a warm bluesiness to their sound, and was used by many of blues guitarists such as Eric Clapton and Jimi Hendrix.



FENDER TELECASTER

The Fender Stratocaster is also lighter and thin sounding guitar, and is also created by Leo Fender.
It is different from the Strat in that it sounds scratchy and twangy, which made it more preferred by country and heartland rock guitarists like Keith Richards and Bruce Springsteen.
