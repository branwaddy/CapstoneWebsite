SOME ICONIC PLAYERS

Over here we go into a few important guitarists in history.

JIMI HENDRIX

“When the power of love overcomes the love of power, the world will know peace.”

Quote by Jimi Hendrix

Mr Hendrix was a guitarist who rose to prominence in the 60s. He was known for his flashy onstage antics and psychedelic guitar tone



Some key characteristics of Jimi and his playing:

  • Left-handed player
  • Frequent use of hammer-ons and pull-offs
    • Combining chords and single note melody
  • Heavy use of fuzz and wah-wah pedals
  • Heavy use of 'wammy' bar
  • Wild and flashy onstage antics such as playing his guitar with his teeth, behind his back and setting it on fire


SLASH

“Guitar is the best form of self-expression I know. Everything else, and I'm just sort of tripping around, trying to figure my way through life.”

Quote by Slash

Slash is one of the most recognizable guitarists in history and rose to prominence in his band, Guns 'N' Roses, which was labelled "the most dangerous band in the world"



Some key characteristics of Slash and his playing:

  • Incredible aptitude for melody
  • Fast and stylistic improvisational legato playing
  • Also known for heavy use of wah pedal
  • Known for the typically gritty rock 'n roll tone of his guitar
  • Played a Gibson Les Paul
  • An incredible mastery of blues and the blues scale


EDDIE VAN HALEN

“Rock stars come and go. Musicians play until they die.”

Quote by Eddie Van Halen

Eddie Van Halen revolutionised the way guitarists played their instrument. He popularised the technique of tapping



Some key characteristics of Eddie and his playing:

  • Popularised 'tapping', a technique involving playing with both of the hands on the fretboard
  • Could play incredibly fast and with even more astounding style and character
  • Was known for using the 'wammy' bar during his techniques called 'divebombs', where he would press it all the way down and the guitar would make a growling noise
  • Frequent use of pinch harmonics
  • Built his own guitar based on the Stratocaster, called the 'Frankenstrat', which was his signature guitar
  • Always had a smile on his face
